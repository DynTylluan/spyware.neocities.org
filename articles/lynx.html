Lynx

From their website: "Lynx is a text browser for the World Wide Web."[1]
Program tested: v2.8.9rel.1 for Linux.
Mitmproxy was used to check for connections.

Spyware Level: Not Spyware

Lynx makes no unsolicited requests at all. It is also fully libre software under the GPLv2 license.
